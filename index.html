Trip Overview

This comprehensive 11-day RV adventure takes you through the best of California, from the beaches of Los Angeles to the majestic mountains of Yosemite and Lake Tahoe, the vibrant city of San Francisco, and the stunning Pacific Coast Highway, concluding with the thrills of Six Flags Magic Mountain.

Your 30' Cruise America Large RV comfortably sleeps 7 people and includes a gas cooktop, refrigerator, microwave, toilet, and shower, making it perfect for this epic California road trip. All driving segments are kept under 5 hours per day as requested, with plenty of time for exploration and relaxation.

Trip Highlights

  • Explore Los Angeles beaches and attractions
  • Experience the natural wonders of Yosemite National Park
  • Enjoy the crystal-clear waters of Lake Tahoe
  • Discover the vibrant city of San Francisco
  • Drive the scenic Pacific Coast Highway
  • Experience thrilling rides at Six Flags Magic Mountain

Key Details

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 (May 2)

Los Angeles Arrival & Setup

  • Land at LAX at 1:00 PM
  • Pick up RV at Cruise America in Carson
  • Shop for supplies at Walmart Supercenter in Long Beach
  • Check in at Dockweiler RV Park
  • Enjoy sunset on the beach

Overnight: Dockweiler RV Park (full hookups)

Driving: 55.2 miles, 1 hour 15 minutes

Day 2 (May 3)

Los Angeles Exploration

  • Explore Santa Monica Pier and Venice Beach
  • Visit Hollywood Walk of Fame
  • Explore Griffith Observatory
  • Return to Dockweiler RV Park

Overnight: Dockweiler RV Park (second night)

Driving: Minimal (use rideshare services)

Day 3 (May 4)

Los Angeles to Bakersfield

  • Check out of Dockweiler RV Park
  • Scenic drive to Bakersfield
  • Check in at Bakersfield River Run RV Park
  • Explore downtown Bakersfield

Overnight: Bakersfield River Run RV Park

Driving: 117 miles, 2 hours 13 minutes

Day 4 (May 5)

Bakersfield to Yosemite

  • Check out of Bakersfield River Run RV Park
  • Scenic drive to Yosemite
  • Lunch stop in Fresno or Oakhurst
  • Check in at Yosemite Pines RV Resort

Overnight: Yosemite Pines RV Resort

Driving: 216 miles, 3 hours 48 minutes

Day 5 (May 6)

Yosemite National Park

  • Drive to Yosemite Valley
  • Visit Yosemite Valley Visitor Center
  • Explore Yosemite Falls, El Capitan, Half Dome
  • Return to Yosemite Pines RV Resort

Overnight: Yosemite Pines RV Resort (second night)

Driving: Local driving within park

Day 6 (May 7)

Yosemite to Lake Tahoe

  • Check out of Yosemite Pines RV Resort
  • Scenic drive to Lake Tahoe
  • Check in at Lake Tahoe KOA
  • Explore South Lake Tahoe shoreline

Overnight: Lake Tahoe KOA

Driving: 149 miles, 3 hours 14 minutes

Day 7 (May 8)

Lake Tahoe to San Francisco

  • Morning at Emerald Bay State Park
  • Check out of Lake Tahoe KOA
  • Scenic drive to San Francisco
  • Check in at San Francisco RV Resort

Overnight: San Francisco RV Resort

Driving: 191 miles, 3 hours 24 minutes

Day 8 (May 9)

San Francisco Exploration

  • Visit Golden Gate Bridge and Golden Gate Park
  • Explore Fisherman's Wharf and Pier 39
  • Optional: Alcatraz tour
  • Return to San Francisco RV Resort

Overnight: San Francisco RV Resort (second night)

Driving: Minimal (use public transportation)

Day 9 (May 10)

San Francisco to Pismo Beach

  • Check out of San Francisco RV Resort
  • Scenic coastal drive on Highway 1
  • Stops at Half Moon Bay, Santa Cruz, Monterey, Big Sur
  • Check in at Pismo Coast Village RV Resort

Overnight: Pismo Coast Village RV Resort

Driving: 242 miles, 3 hours 36 minutes

Day 10 (May 11)

Pismo Beach to Valencia

  • Check out of Pismo Coast Village RV Resort
  • Drive to Valencia with lunch stop in Santa Barbara
  • Check in at Valencia Travel Village
  • Prepare for Six Flags visit

Overnight: Valencia Travel Village

Driving: 152 miles, 2 hours 32 minutes

Day 11 (May 12)

Six Flags & Return

  • Check out of Valencia Travel Village
  • Full day at Six Flags Magic Mountain
  • Late afternoon: Return RV to Cruise America in Carson

Driving: 45 miles, 50 minutes
